1. Information Collection

Hydrate does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device only.

2. Local Data Storage

The app uses a local database (SQLite) to store the following information:

  • Daily water intake records (time, amount)
  • App settings (goals, reminder times)

This data is stored only on your device and is never uploaded to any server.

3. Permissions

The app only requests the following permission:

  • Notification permission: Used to send hydration reminders. You can disable this at any time in your system settings.

We do not request any other permissions such as location, camera, or contacts.

4. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally, your data security is protected by your device's operating system. We recommend:

  • Regularly backing up your device
  • Using a screen lock password
  • Note: Uninstalling the app will delete all local data

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since the app does not collect any data, users of all ages can safely use it.

7. Health Disclaimer

This app is intended for tracking and reminders only and does not provide medical advice. Water intake suggestions are for reference only. Please consult a healthcare professional for specific needs. Using this app is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be notified through app updates. We recommend reviewing this policy periodically.
